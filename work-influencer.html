Short-Form Collaborations

Alongside my own content, I've produced short-form videos in direct collaboration with influencers and other creators — handling everything from concept and filming through to the final edit. Several individual pieces from these collaborations have each reached over 1 million views, built on the same fundamentals that drive any strong short-form video: a hook in the first second, pacing that respects the platform, and a format built to be watched all the way through.

A Larger Production: Course & VSL

My biggest collaboration in this space scaled well beyond a single video. Working directly with a creator client, I took on scripting, filming, and editing for a full digital course — then carried that same production into the video sales letter (VSL) used to launch it. That meant staying consistent on message, pacing, and visual identity across dozens of pieces of content rather than one standalone video, while keeping the course itself clear, watchable, and built to actually get finished.

Approach

Whatever the format, the process stays the same: understand the platform and the audience first, write and shoot for the hook, then edit with the analytics in mind rather than just instinct. That feedback loop — what people actually watch, share, and finish — is what shapes the next piece of content, and it's the thread that connects a single viral short to a full course built to convert.
